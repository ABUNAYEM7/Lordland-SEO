Statement of Faith

Our Beliefs

  • God – We believe in one Holy Triune God existing eternally in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.
  • God The Father – Governs all things and accomplishes His purposes in creation and the Church to His glory.
  • Jesus Christ – True God and fully man, Savior of the world, who was incarnated by the Holy Spirit and born of the Virgin Mary.
  • The Holy Spirit – Indwells, regenerates, sanctifies, and empowers believers to love, serve, and obey God.
  • Holy Bible – The final authority, infallible rule, and divine standard for Christian faith and practice.
  • Creation – The universe was created as presented in the Bible, including the existence of heaven, hell, and spiritual realities.
  • Human Beings – Created in God's image, but born into sin due to Adam and Eve’s disobedience.
  • Salvation – Comes through Jesus Christ; those who accept Him as Savior receive eternal life.
  • Sanctity and Life – Every human life is sacred and should be fully dedicated to God's will.
  • The Church – Consists of all believers in Jesus Christ, commissioned to spread the Gospel.
  • Healing – Divine healing and miracles are gifts received according to God's will and purpose.
  • Resurrection – Both the just and unjust will be resurrected; the just to eternal life, the unjust to judgment.
  • The Second Advent – The imminent second coming of Jesus Christ is the blessed hope of all believers.

About Lordland University

Lordland University is dedicated to providing innovative and transformative education with a strong foundation in Christian ethics. Our mission is to empower students to become ethical leaders in their fields.
