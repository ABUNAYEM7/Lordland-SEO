SEVIS I-20 for International Students

Lordland College

Lordland College issues a Form I-20 to qualifying international students. Students who wish to apply for a Form I-20 must provide proof of financial resources in order to enroll at Lordland College.

The proof of financial resources or aid should cover both educational and living costs, which are approximately $30,000 per year.

Students must also submit all necessary documents for the Form I-20 to be processed. Enrollment will be processed once the application and supporting documents—listed below—have been submitted to the Office of the Registrar.

Required Documents

International students from other countries must submit the following documents:

  • Application for admissions
  • An official transcript and/or diploma from previously attended schools
  • Recommendation Letter (from Pastor, Professor, Leader, etc.)
  • Personal Statement
  • 2 Passport Photos
  • Recent Bank Statement (Financial Support)
  • A copy of Passport
  • A copy of your VISA and I-94 (both front and back)
  • Application Fee: $200
