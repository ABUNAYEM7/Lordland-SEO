Since 1890, Lordland University has been a beacon of academic excellence, nurturing innovation and critical thinking. Our institution empowers students and faculty to shape the future through transformative learning experiences.

With a strong foundation in interdisciplinary collaboration, our research breakthroughs push the boundaries of knowledge and real-world impact. We strive to create a dynamic environment that fosters growth, inclusivity, and global perspectives.

President's Welcome

“All Scripture is God-breathed...” (2 Timothy 3:16-17)

It is a pleasure to welcome you to Lordland University. Lordland University has been established to brighten the torch of the truth in this dark world, through working with you.

The purpose of our institution is to develop the power of God which has bestowed to the human being and to transmit the superpower of the subconsciousness to the human being.

Our university offers the highest quality education by integrating educational experiences both inside and outside of the classroom. Our faculty members are experienced professionals and doctors in many fields who are committed to help you in order to create an encouraging academic environment. You will surely find welcoming and caring faculty members and staff who are designated to support your educational journey here at Lordland University.

Kang I. Lee, Ph.D. & J.D.
President
