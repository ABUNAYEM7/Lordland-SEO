Master of Science in Business Administration (MSBA)

Lordland University, USA

Program Overview

The Master of Science in Business Administration (MSBA) at Lordland University is designed to develop business leaders with strong analytical, strategic, and ethical decision-making skills. Rooted in Christian values, the program emphasizes integrity, servant leadership, and social responsibility in business operations.

The curriculum integrates advanced business theories with practical applications to prepare students for senior management roles. Special attention is given to global business challenges, innovation, digital transformation, and ethical leadership, ensuring that graduates can navigate the complexities of modern business while upholding Christian moral principles.

Graduation Requirements

  • Complete 64 quarter units (16 courses).
  • Maintain a minimum CGPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Complete the program within 1.5 times the program length (normally 8 quarters).
  • File all required graduation forms with the School of Business Management and Office of Admissions and Records.
  • Settle all financial obligations to the university.

Christian Perspective in MSBA Program

  • Faith-Integrated Learning: Courses include Christian business ethics, servant leadership, and moral decision-making in business.
  • Christian-Based Leadership: Emphasis on ethical leadership, community service, and socially responsible business models inspired by Biblical principles.
  • Ethical & Social Responsibility: Encourages students to use business as a platform to serve society, promote justice, and honor God in their professional endeavors.

Program Outcomes (PLOs)

  1. Demonstrate Leadership & Strategic Thinking: Lead organizations effectively with a vision-driven approach based on Christian values.
  2. Analyze & Solve Business Problems: Apply critical thinking and data analysis to tackle complex challenges.
  3. Exhibit Ethical Decision-Making: Make business decisions rooted in integrity, honesty, and social responsibility.
  4. Apply Global Business Insights: Understand international business strategies while integrating Christian ethical standards.
  5. Master Business Technologies: Utilize AI, data analytics, and cybersecurity tools to drive innovation and growth.
  6. Develop Entrepreneurial & Innovative Mindsets: Launch and manage ethical businesses that contribute to economic development and social well-being.

Program Duration & Timeframe

Standard Completion Time: 8 quarters (2 years)
Maximum Timeframe (MTF): 12 quarters (3 years)
