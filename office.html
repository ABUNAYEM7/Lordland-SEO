Overview

The Lordland University administrative staff is here to support and serve students in many ways. Whether related to the admission process, orienting new students, academic advising, or meeting graduation requirements, our team is dedicated to providing outstanding, learning-centered service and educational opportunities.

From simple requests for information to more confidential matters, our staff gives students the time and space to talk things through. To view IAU’s instructional faculty, click here.

Whether you are a student or a staff member, Lordland University is dedicated to providing the best opportunities while on campus. Prospective students, current students, and alumni can stay connected with the IAU community—because learning is a lifelong journey.

Students in need of personal guidance can schedule an appointment with our staff for help with graduation, I-20 issues, finance, career advice, and more.
