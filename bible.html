Biblical Code of Conduct

"But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, forbearance, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness and self-control..." (Galatians 5:22-23)

Expectations for Followers of Christ

  • Manifest the fruit of the Spirit (Gal. 5:22-23; Col. 3:12-14).
  • Seek righteousness, mercy, and justice (Prov. 21:3; Micah 6:8).
  • Uphold human dignity and the sanctity of marriage (Gen. 1:27; Heb. 13:4).
  • Practice wise stewardship of resources (Gal. 5:13-14).
  • Live a peaceful, prayerful, and Godly life (1 Thess. 4:11; Heb. 10:25).
  • Be a witness for the Gospel and resolve conflicts biblically (Acts 1:8; Matt. 18:15-17).

Prohibited Behaviors

  • Blasphemy, lying, stealing, dishonesty, and gossip (Exod. 20:7; Col. 3:8-9).
  • Sexual immorality, idolatry, and jealousy (Gal. 5:19-21).
  • Adultery, fornication, and all sexual relations outside biblical marriage (Lev. 18:22; Rom. 1:21-27).

Students at Lordland University are expected to adhere to these biblical principles in all aspects of their lives, both within and outside the university community.

About Lordland University

Lordland University is dedicated to providing innovative and transformative education with a strong foundation in Christian ethics. Our mission is to empower students to become ethical leaders in their fields.
