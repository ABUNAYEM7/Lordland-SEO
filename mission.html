Our Mission

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age." (Matthew 28:19-20)

Our mission is to offer exemplary education through our undergraduate and graduate degrees in Theology, Religious Education, and Missiology. We strive to cultivate in students:

  • Intellectual and academic integrity, along with informal ethical values
  • Tolerance of social differences and diversity of cultures
  • Creativity in critical thinking and intellectual problem-solving
  • Personal, professional, and social leadership skills
  • A sense of personal joy and fulfillment
  • Sensitivity to the needs of others and commitment to the betterment of humanity

We achieve our mission through excellence in teaching and public service, emphasizing:

  • Maintaining a learning environment that attracts outstanding students
  • Preparing students for success in professional programs
  • Providing a physical environment and support services that help students reach their educational goals

About Lordland University

Lordland University is dedicated to providing innovative and transformative education with a strong foundation in Christian ethics. Our mission is to empower students to become ethical leaders in their fields.
